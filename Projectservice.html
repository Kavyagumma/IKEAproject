We care about your safety!

All goods are picked, packed and sent from the IKEA store in Hyderabad. We have taken all possible precautions to secure safe deliveries:
• All premises are cleaned with additional frequency using disinfectants
• Temperature is checked on all co-workers before entering the store
• All co-workers will go through a full day training in safety
• All co-workers maintain a distance of 1.5 metres at all points of time
• All co-workers wear masks full-time and latex gloves when handling products
• All trucks are fully disinfected before and after delivery rounds

Work with us.Together we can make a difference.

We can deliver your IKEA orders to your home or workplace. We can deliver to your front door, or we can take your delivery items all the way into your room of choice.


Online DeliveryAhmedabad can now place small orders (less than 20KG).Buy from our full online range in Hyderabad, Mumbai and Pune.


Please note:Delivery excludes plants, food, and fragile objects and articles from AS-IS. For AS-IS deliveries please speak to a member of staff in our AS-IS section.


Kitchen Delivery Costs

Our delivery price for kitchens is Rs.1,999 and can be delivered within 2 to 30 days.This price includes storage up to the agreed delivery date for up to 30 days.
From 31 days a storage charge of Rs. 250 per day applies.
